What is MindLoops® Maker Space?

A hands-on collaborative program that brings together like-minded students to co-develop a prototype by using concepts from STEM and technical skills from TVET.

How can I participate?

  • Simply form a group of 5 from members of your school to participate. Remember to bring your teacher to support you!
  • Work together with your team to develop the idea and concept to produce a prototype based on the themes selected.
  • Submit your brilliant idea and concept for our juries to assess.
  • Selected teams will be invited to join the main event.
  • Unleash your creativity by developing the prototype on the event day.
  • Present your wonderful prototype to our judges.
  • Winners will be awarded prizes!
